AI: Go with **left-aligned bullet points**. That’s the standard for a reason.

---

### Why left-aligned is more professional

* **Easier to read** → your eye tracks from the same starting point every line
* **Consistent structure** → everything lines up cleanly
* **Used everywhere** → slides from companies like Apple and Microsoft almost always do this

---

### Why centered bullets don’t work well

* Each line starts in a different place → harder to scan quickly
* Looks less structured, more like a poster than a presentation
* Fine for **titles or single short statements**, but not lists

---

### Simple rule

* **Lists = left-aligned**
* **Titles / short one-liners = can be centered**

---

If you want it to look extra clean:

* Keep bullets short (1 line if possible)
* Align all text to the same left margin
* Add a bit of spacing between bullets so it doesn’t feel cramped
